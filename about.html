Shehraz Riar

Founder & Chief Editor

Shehraz founded terroir to bridge the gap between high fashion consumption and the lived realities of the communities whose cultures are constantly commodified on the runway.

With a critical eye on the geopolitical flows of textile production and design inspiration, Shehraz uses this platform to return the narrative focus to the true originating artisans of the world's most lauded silhouettes.
