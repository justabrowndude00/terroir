Ginew

Native American Owned (Ojibwe, Oneida, Mohican)

The first Native American-owned denim collection. They incorporate family symbols and teachings into their garments, utilizing natural materials like selvedge denim and Pendleton wool blanket fabric.

Founded by Bethany Yellowtail, this brand prioritizes authentic Indigenous design, community, and culture. They offer intricately patterned apparel made by a collective of Native American artisans.
